Privately Guided National Park Journeys
in California

California Journeys is a Luxury Adventure Travel Company dedicated to curating exceptional journeys for discerning travelers around California.

Our mission is to craft tailor-made experiences that combine adventure and comfort. Every trip is designed to immerse you in California's most extraordinary destinations while ensuring seamless service from start to finish.

With decades of combined expertise in high-end adventure travel, our team has built strong relationships with the finest lodges, private villas, exclusive safari camps, private guides, local outfitters and service providers.

We believe in creating experiences that are as enriching as they are indulgent, prioritizing environmental stewardship, cultural respect, and meaningful connections.

Integrity, excellence, and personalization are at the heart of everything we do.
